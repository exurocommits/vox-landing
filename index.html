Why Vox

Built for people who type all day

🔒

100% Local

Powered by whisper.cpp running directly on your GPU. Your voice never leaves your machine. No API keys, no cloud services, no monthly fees. Works completely offline.

⚡

GPU-Accelerated

Uses NVIDIA CUDA for transcription faster than real-time — about 50× on an RTX 3060. CPU fallback available for machines without a GPU.

🎯

Text Injection

Your words type directly into whatever app has focus. VS Code, Slack, Notion, Google Docs, browser address bars — anywhere you'd type, Vox types for you.

💊

Floating Widget

A minimal always-on-top pill, 220×72px. Shows recording state, transcription results, and stays out of your way. Draggable to any position.

🧠

Smart Silence Detection

Voice Activity Detection (VAD) stops recording when you stop talking. No manual stop needed. Just speak naturally and Vox handles the rest.

📜

Transcription History

Every transcription saved locally with search. Browse, copy, or reuse past transcriptions. 1,000 entry history cap. Nothing leaves your machine.

How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

01

Press your hotkey

Default is Win+H, fully customizable. Click the mic button on the floating widget, or use push-to-talk mode. Vox starts listening instantly.

02

Just talk

Speak naturally. Vox captures audio, runs Voice Activity Detection, and processes speech in 2.5-second chunks for live streaming results.

03

Text appears everywhere

Your transcription is injected directly into your active window and copied to clipboard. The widget shows results with a smooth scramble animation, then auto-dismisses.
